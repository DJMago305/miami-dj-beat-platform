# MIAMI DJ BEAT PLATFORM — OFFICIAL PAYMENT POLICY

## 1. DEPOSIT POLICY
All event bookings require a thirty percent (30%) initial deposit to secure the event date and the assigned DJ's availability. This deposit is strictly non-refundable once the event state transitions to **BOOKED**. Payment of the deposit constitutes the Client's acceptance of the event requirements as defined in the current Version of the Event Blueprint.

## 2. FINAL PAYMENT POLICY
The remaining seventy percent (70%) balance of the total event value will be automatically charged to the Client's registered payment method exactly seventy-two (72) hours (T-3 days) prior to the scheduled event start time. Successful collection of this balance transitions the event to the **PAID_FULL** state. Failure to collect the final payment may result in immediate cancellation of the event and forfeiture of the initial deposit.

## 3. BALANCED CANCELLATION MATRIX
The following outcomes apply based on the party initiating the cancellation and the current state of the event:

| Current Event State | Initiating Party | Client Financial Outcome | Platform/DJ Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **REQUESTED / MATCHED** | Any | 0% Cost. No charges processed. | No ledger impact. **MATCHED Expiration**: Auto-expires in 24h if no deposit paid. |
| **BOOKED** (T > 3 days) | Client | 30% Deposit Forfeited. | Retained by Platform/DJ. |
| **BOOKED** (T > 3 days) | Platform / DJ | 100% Refund of Deposit. | Penalty applied to DJ. |
| **PAID_FULL** (T ≤ 3 days) | Client | 30% Deposit + 50% Final Payment Forfeited (50% Final Payment Refunded). | Partial DJ Payout. |
| **PAID_FULL** (T ≤ 3 days) | Platform / DJ | 100% Total Refund. | Manual re-match priority. |
| **IN_PROGRESS** | Any | No Refund. | Full Payout executed. |

## 4. DJ FAILURE & REPLACEMENT PROTOCOL
In the unlikely event of DJ incapacitation or failure to perform (DJ-initiated cancellation or "No-Show"), the Platform will initiate an emergency re-matching process to secure a replacement DJ of equal or higher compatibility. If no suitable replacement can be found, the Client is entitled to a full one hundred percent (100%) refund of all funds paid.

## 5. ANTI-CIRCUMVENTION CLAUSE & LIQUIDATED DAMAGES
DJs registered on the Miami DJ Beat Platform are strictly prohibited from soliciting, accepting, or performing services for a Client introduced via the Platform outside of the Platform’s payment systems. Any DJ found in violation of this clause will be permanently de-platformed. 

**Liquidated Damages**: Breach of this clause triggers a penalty of two times (2x) the total event value or three thousand dollars ($3,000.00), whichever is greater. By registering, the DJ provides **explicit authorization** for the Platform to deduct these damages directly from any pending or future Payouts or stored ledger balances.

## 6. CLIENT OFF-PLATFORM DISCLAIMER
Clients who agree to engage or pay a DJ directly outside of the Miami DJ Beat Platform do so at their own exclusive risk. The Platform **assumes zero liability and provides no protections**, including but not limited to: Refund Guarantees, Quality Assurance, DJ Replacement Protocols, and Dispute Resolution services. Any off-platform arrangement constitutes a total waiver of all Platform-backed security and service commitments.
