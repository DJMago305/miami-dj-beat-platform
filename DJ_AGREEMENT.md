# MIAMI DJ BEAT PLATFORM — DJ SERVICE AGREEMENT

This DJ Service Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into by and between the DJ ("Contractor") and Miami DJ Beat Platform ("Platform"). By registering an account and accepting event bookings via the Platform, Contractor agrees to be legally bound by these terms.

## 1. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS
The Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, or agent of the Platform. Contractor is solely responsible for the manner and means of performing the services, as well as for all taxes, insurance, and equipment necessary for performance.

## 2. PLATFORM RELATIONSHIP DEFINITION
The Platform acts as a marketplace and technology intermediary that connects Clients with DJs. The Platform does not employ DJs and is not responsible for the performance or conduct of the Contractor or the Client.

## 3. PAYMENT STRUCTURE
Payments for services shall strictly follow the Platform’s payment methodology:
- **Deposit**: A thirty percent (30%) non-refundable deposit collected from the Client at the time of booking.
- **Final Payment**: A seventy percent (70%) balance collected three (3) days prior to the event date.
- **Payout**: Net payout (Total Event Value minus Platform Commission) will be released to the Contractor following successful completion of the event and confirmation of services.

## 4. DJ CANCELLATION OBLIGATIONS
Contractor agrees that the booking of an event date on the Platform constitutes a firm commitment. Any cancellation by the Contractor after the event state has transitioned to **BOOKED** is considered a material breach of this Agreement unless excused by Force Majeure.

## 5. DJ FAILURE & REPLACEMENT LIABILITY
In the event of Contractor’s failure to perform or unauthorized cancellation, Contractor shall be liable for any costs incurred by the Platform in securing an emergency replacement DJ. If the Platform is forced to issue a refund to the Client due to Contractor's failure, Contractor shall indemnify the Platform for the lost commission and any associated damages.

## 6. ANTI-CIRCUMVENTION
Contractor agrees that for a period of twenty-four (24) months following the introduction of a Client via the Platform, Contractor shall not solicit, negotiate, or accept any engagement from said Client, or any associate of said Client, outside of the Platform’s payment and matching systems.

## 7. LIQUIDATED DAMAGES
Due to the difficulty of quantifying damages to the Platform’s marketplace integrity, brand reputation, and lost revenue, Contractor agrees that any violation of the Anti-Circumvention clause shall trigger a Liquidated Damages penalty. Contractor shall pay the Platform the greater of: (a) two times (2x) the total value of the circumvented event, or (b) three thousand dollars ($3,000.00).

## 8. AUTHORIZATION FOR AUTOMATIC DEDUCTION
Contractor hereby provides **explicit and irrevocable authorization** for the Platform to deduct any Liquidated Damages, cancellation penalties, or indemnity amounts directly from the Contractor’s pending payouts, future earnings, or any funds held in the Contractor’s Platform ledger.

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY
Contractor shall maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information of the Platform and any sensitive data provided by Clients (including contact information and event logistics). This obligation survives the termination of this Agreement.

## 10. NON-DISPARAGEMENT
Contractor agrees not to make any false, disparaging, or derogatory statements (written or oral) regarding the Platform, its employees, or its services on social media, public forums, or to Clients.

## 11. FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to causes beyond their reasonable control, including but not limited to: natural disasters, war, government mandates, or civil unrest. Contractor must notify the Platform within four (4) hours of the occurrence of a Force Majeure event.

## 12. TERMINATION & SUSPENSION RIGHTS
The Platform reserves the right to suspend or terminate Contractor’s access to the marketplace at any time, with or without cause. Grounds for immediate termination include, but are not limited to: circumvention, poor performance ratings, or breach of this Agreement.

## 13. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida, USA, without regard to its conflict of law principles.

## 14. DISPUTE RESOLUTION & ARBITRATION
Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Miami-Dade County, Florida. The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney’s fees and costs.

## 15. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
The Platform’s total liability to the Contractor for any claim arising out of this Agreement shall not exceed the total commission earned by the Platform from events performed by the Contractor in the six (6) months preceding the claim.

## 16. ELECTRONIC SIGNATURE & ACCEPTANCE
Contractor acknowledges that by clicking "Accept," "Register," or by accepting any event booking via the Platform, Contractor is providing an electronic signature and agrees to be legally bound by the terms of this Agreement as if it were signed in ink.
