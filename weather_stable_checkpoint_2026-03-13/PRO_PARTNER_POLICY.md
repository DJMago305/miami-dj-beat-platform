# MIAMI DJ BEAT PLATFORM — PRO PARTNER POLICY (FINAL VERSION)

This Pro Partner Policy (the “Policy”) governs the terms under which a Professional DJ (“Pro Partner”) participates in the Miami DJ Beat Platform Pro Partner Program (the “Program”). This Policy supplements and incorporates the DJ Service Agreement and Official Payment Policy. In the event of conflict, the DJ Service Agreement shall control.

Enrollment in the Program is voluntary and subject to continuous compliance with all Platform agreements.

## 1. MONTHLY SUBSCRIPTION

Pro Partner status requires payment of a non-refundable monthly subscription fee of ninety-nine dollars ($99.00 USD).

The subscription fee provides access to enhanced marketplace privileges, including but not limited to:

- Eligibility to accept Premium and Corporate Events  
- Priority consideration within the Matching Engine  
- Access to automated contract execution tools  
- Access to advanced platform tools and analytics  
- Conditional eligibility for the Corporate Liability Coverage Program  

The subscription fee compensates access to infrastructure, enhanced exposure, and operational privileges. It does not guarantee event assignments, booking volume, or income.

Failure to maintain active subscription status results in immediate suspension of Pro Partner privileges.

## 2. COMMISSION STRUCTURE — ANNUAL TIER SYSTEM

Commission is calculated as a percentage of Gross Booking Value (“GBV”) processed through the Platform.

Tiers are determined annually based on total GBV generated during the prior calendar year:

- Tier 1 (Starter Pro): $0 – $25,000 GBV → 22% commission  
- Tier 2 (Growth Pro): $25,001 – $75,000 GBV → 18% commission  
- Tier 3 (Elite Pro): $75,001+ GBV → 15% commission  

All commissions are deducted automatically from event payouts prior to disbursement.

## 3. ANNUAL TIER REVIEW PROCESS

Tier assignment occurs annually on January 1st.

The Pro Partner’s GBV from the immediately preceding calendar year determines commission rate for the upcoming year.

Tier rates remain fixed for the entire calendar year and shall not be downgraded mid-year, provided the Pro Partner remains in good standing.

Tier adjustments are not applied retroactively.

## 4. CORPORATE LIABILITY COVERAGE PROGRAM (CONDITIONAL)

Active Pro Partners may be eligible for inclusion in the Platform’s Event Liability Coverage Program for specific events booked and processed exclusively through the Platform.

Eligibility is:

- Event-specific  
- Subject to internal approval  
- Subject to underwriting review  
- Conditional upon compliance with all Platform policies  

The Platform is not an insurance carrier. Coverage inclusion does not eliminate the Pro Partner’s primary responsibility for professional conduct or negligence.

The Platform reserves the right to deny coverage inclusion for any event at its sole discretion.

Failure to report any incident within twenty-four (24) hours may result in denial of coverage eligibility.

## 5. CREDIT PROTECTION MODEL

If during a full calendar month:

- The Pro Partner maintains active status  
- Profile and availability are fully updated  
- No cancellations or policy violations occur  
- No event proposals are received through the Platform  

The Platform may issue a discretionary service credit of fifty dollars ($50.00 USD) applicable to the following billing cycle.

This credit:

- Is not automatic  
- Is not a refund  
- Is not guaranteed  
- Is subject to Platform validation  

The Platform retains sole discretion in determining eligibility.

## 6. FREEZE OPTION

Pro Partners may request to freeze their subscription for up to sixty (60) total days per calendar year, limited to two (2) separate freeze periods.

Freeze conditions:

- Must be requested prior to billing cycle renewal  
- Cannot be activated while assigned to active booked events  
- No retroactive freeze  
- Matching privileges and Program benefits are suspended during freeze  

Freeze periods do not extend Tier evaluation periods.

## 7. NO GUARANTEE OF EVENT VOLUME

The Platform provides marketplace infrastructure and matching technology but does not guarantee event volume, booking frequency, or income levels.

Event assignments depend on Client demand, competition, seasonality, ratings, availability, and operational factors.

## 8. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

To maintain Pro Partner status, DJs must:

- Maintain minimum rating thresholds  
- Maintain compliance with Anti-Circumvention obligations  
- Avoid excessive cancellations  
- Maintain professional conduct standards  
- Cooperate with Platform investigations  
- Remain current on subscription payments  

Failure to meet these conditions may result in suspension or termination.

## 9. DISCRETION & ENFORCEMENT

The Platform reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to:

- Suspend Pro Partner privileges  
- Freeze payouts during investigation  
- Revoke Tier status  
- Deny event eligibility  
- Modify Program terms with reasonable notice  

Waiver of enforcement in one instance does not establish precedent.

## 10. PROGRAM MODIFICATION

The Platform reserves the right to modify this Policy with notice to active Pro Partners. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of modifications.
